Creature Types

● Plant — eats from terrain, reproduces fast
▲ Hunter — kills plants and weak creatures
◆ Apex — top predator, kills hunters
✚ Scavenger — eats corpses, follows trails
✦ Parasite — boosts hosts, steals waste
⬢ Swarm — shares energy, clusters
➤ Nomad — fast, avoids fights

How Evolution Shows

▬▬ Size changes — genes make creatures grow bigger (more armor) or shrink (more speed) over generations
●● Color diverges — different lineages drift apart in color as their DNA mutates differently
■■■■ Trait pip — tiny dot on creature = dominant gene: speed armor harvest sense
○ Gen ring — outline = old lineage (50+ gen = thick ring, success!)
○ Birth ring — expanding circle = new offspring born
▪▪▪ Death scatter — red particles = creature died

Environment Cues

■ Green glow under plant = actively feeding
■ Red glow under predator = hunting mode
■ Bright green terrain = food abundant
■ Brown terrain = depleted by grazing
■ Golden paths = creature highways (wear)
■ Amber trails = pheromone activity
■ Red-brown stains = battle/death zone
◆ Season flash = green/gold/orange/blue pulse when season changes

Weather

Rain — +food, wets terrain
Fog — halves sensing
Snow — -food, whitens land
Storm — lightning, dark, dangerous
Drought — food dries up fast
Heatwave — high energy cost
Weather changes with seasons

Terrain

█ Grass — lots of food grows
█ Forest — food + hiding from hunters
█ Dirt — some food
█ Sand — barely any food
█ Water — impassable barrier
█ Rock — no food, walkable
